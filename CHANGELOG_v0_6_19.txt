VERA v0.6.19

User-facing terminology
- Uses the full title Open British Arboricultural Record Dataset (OpenBARD).
- Uses the full title Veteran Architecture Index (VAI).
- Uses the full title Veteran Tree Evidence Matrix (VTEM).
- Expanded titles are used in assessment screens, evidence chains, PNG summaries, report text and feedback diagnostics.

Evidence-chain presentation correction
- An ATF mature result remains in Ancient status and no longer appears as an incomplete evidence chain.
- OpenBARD Large, Very large and Exceptional candidate results remain in Size evidence and no longer appear as incomplete evidence chains.
- Emerging or progressing Veteran Architecture Index results remain condition observations and no longer appear as incomplete evidence chains.
- Genuine review and supported chains remain visible.

Unchanged
- ATF, FCIN12, OpenBARD lookup, Veteran Architecture Index and Veteran Tree Evidence Matrix calculation rules.
- Heritage/cultural inputs, notes handling, feedback workflow and botanical graphics.
