- Share with files passes the JSON and generated PNG to a supported device share sheet.
- Prepare email downloads the files and opens a pre-addressed email draft.
- Fully automatic delivery is available when a secure HTTPS feedback endpoint is configured.
- The existing VERA PNG export uses html2canvas 1.4.1 from its existing CDN reference, so PNG creation requires that library to load.
